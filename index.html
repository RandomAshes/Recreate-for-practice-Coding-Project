Join Nutanix

Nutanix is looking for passionate Product Designers to join their ranks.
Help us shape the human interfaces used in the data centers of the future.

DESIGN FIRST

As a Product Designer, you will be involved in every aspect of the product development process, from brainstorming in the early feature concepts to tweaking pixels right before launch. As a result, you will be highly involved in each step of our product creation. You will be able to fully use your range of product design, interaction design, and visual design skills, which will allow you to own the 'experience' for a wide area of the Nutanix product.

MODERN DESIGN

We are using every modern aspect of web based application design. Simplicity and usability are key. But also technology. Our html5 based application is fully responsive, uses modern typography and is following the modern usability trends dictated by the industry.

RESPONSIBILITIES

Take conceptual ideas related to virtual data management and turn them into something useful, simple and beautiful.

Design flows and experiences that are incredibly simple and elegant.

Work closely with the visual design team to build a highly engaging UI that predicts and exceeds user expectations in HTML5, Javascript and CSS.

Contribute to high-level strategic decisions with the rest of the product and executive teams.

Partner with PMs, engineers & researchers to oversee the user experience of new features and products from conception until launch.

REQUIREMENTS

Flat, skeu, white space and PS layer styles have no secret for you, but you also strive for problem solving making complex interfaces easy and simple to use.

The ability to think at a high level about product strategy, not just 'how it should look' but 'what we should build to make it simple'.

You possess excellent verbal and written communication skills. You should be able to clearly articulate your design decisions.

Experience in building and shipping applications or software.

Must have a portfolio featuring examples of interaction design work.

Some experience with front-end programming is a plus.

This is a job based in our HQ. Must be willing to relocate to sunny San Francisco South Bay (San Jose).
